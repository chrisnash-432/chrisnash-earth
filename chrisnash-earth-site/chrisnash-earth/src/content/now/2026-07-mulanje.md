---
title: "Mulanje's next chapter"
date: 2026-07-10
place: "Mulanje, Malawi"
---

Mulanje steps into its new status as a UNESCO World Heritage Cultural Landscape, and the work is evolving with it: from restoration projects toward a bioregional strategy, restoration-linked value chains like beekeeping and dried fruit, and the operational backbone for a scalable restoration hub, in partnership with Mulanje Mission Hospital, DHL Africa and a wide local coalition.
