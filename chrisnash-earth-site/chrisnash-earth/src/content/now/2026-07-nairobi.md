title: "Building Nairobi's syntropic flagship"
date: 2026-07-15
place: "Nairobi, Kenya"

We're launching a flagship syntropic-agroforestry demonstration site and training centre in Nairobi with Greenpop and DHL Africa: high-density, highly productive restoration that works with nature, designed from day one to be replicated across the continent. The coalition matters more than the method; agroforestry experts, community-led NGOs, researchers and funders are all invited in.
